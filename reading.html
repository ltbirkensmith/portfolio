All-Time Favorites

Clean Architecture

by Robert C. Martin

A masterclass in software architecture principles. Martin's insights on separation of concerns and dependency management are foundational to building maintainable systems.

★★★★★

The Phoenix Project

by Gene Kim, Kevin Behr & George Spafford

A novel about DevOps that reads like a thriller. Illustrates the transformative power of proper software development practices and organizational change.

★★★★★

Thinking, Fast and Slow

by Daniel Kahneman

Kahneman's exploration of cognitive biases is essential reading for anyone making decisions—which is to say, everyone. Particularly relevant for engineering trade-offs.
