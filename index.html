About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary engineer with a passion for creating innovative solutions that bridge technology and creativity. My work has spanned NASA Competitions and several organizations at Columbia

With a foundation in engineering principles and a creative approach to problem-solving, I specialize in developing robust systems and elegant solutions. My projects range from complex technical implementations to visual storytelling through photography.

I believe in the power of interdisciplinary thinking, where technical precision meets creative exploration. This philosophy drives my approach to every project, whether I'm architecting software systems, capturing moments through a lens, or articulating ideas through writing.
